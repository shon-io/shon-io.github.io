Your Technology Should Serve You

 

It really is a straightforward as the title. When you use an app, whether on your phone, tablet, laptop, or PC, it should be helping you to do work, not insisting that you change how you work.

There are some sacrifices of convenience we must make, like longer passwords and multi-factor authentication, but when you get to the heart of the task, technology should be helping, not hindering.

If your tech is getting in the way, I do my best to make your current systems serve you better. Or, I can design a complete system that will suit your needs and make you and your team more productive.
